Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Overview

NV Doctors Placements ("we", "us", "our") operates as a medical recruitment agency connecting hospitals, medical colleges and doctors across India. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and protect information shared with us through WhatsApp, email, phone, and this website.

Information We Collect

We may collect information you voluntarily share with us, including but not limited to:

  • Name, contact number, email address and location
  • Professional qualifications, resumes/CVs, and career history (for doctors)
  • Hiring requirements, department details and role specifications (for hospitals)
  • Communication history exchanged via WhatsApp, email or phone

This website does not use contact or application forms. Information is shared with us directly by you, through WhatsApp, email, or phone, at your own initiative.

How We Use Information

Information shared with us is used solely to:

  • Match doctors with relevant hospital or medical college opportunities
  • Match hospitals with qualified, verified candidates
  • Coordinate interviews, negotiations, and the joining process
  • Provide after-placement and replacement support
  • Respond to general enquiries about our services

Confidentiality

Confidentiality is core to how we operate. Candidate information is never shared publicly or listed openly, and hospital requirements are shared only with candidates relevant to that specific mandate, with appropriate discretion.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information is shared only between the relevant hospital and candidate as part of an active recruitment process, and only to the extent necessary to facilitate that process.

Data Retention

We retain candidate and client information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide recruitment services, or until you request its removal.

Your Rights

Third-Party Links

This website may contain links to third-party platforms such as WhatsApp. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of our services after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact Us
